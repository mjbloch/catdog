About CatDog

CatDog is an image classification model for your four-legged friends.

When an image is uploaded, the model will analyze the image and predict whether the image is a cat or a dog.

Note, the model does not work with any other images besides cats or dogs.

Please proceed to the "Predict" tab to upload your image and receive the model's prediction.
